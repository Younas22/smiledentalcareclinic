About Smile Dental Care Clinic

Smile Dental Care Clinic is a trusted dental clinic providing quality oral healthcare for patients of all ages. Led by a dentist with 7 years of experience, we offer dental implants, fixed and removable braces, zirconia crowns, root canal treatment, scaling and polishing, dental fillings, flexible dentures, laser teeth whitening, and complete smile restoration.

We focus on patient comfort, modern dental techniques, and personalized treatment plans to help you achieve a healthy and confident smile. Conveniently located in Kacha Khuh near Dr. Zawar Hussain Shah Clinic, Phattak Chowk, we proudly serve local residents and nearby communities with professional and reliable dental care.
